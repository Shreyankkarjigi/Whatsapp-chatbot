Notable Features



Self diagnosing quiz(Beta)



Self diagnosing quiz let's you keep track of your health easily,know whether you have any symptoms matching covid.



User Friendly Menu



An user friendly menu system that shows all the options available to user,user just have to reply with respective codes to view more information.



Donate



Donate to various charity right through the bot,we have handpicked the best NGO's out there that are working relentlessly to help less priveleged people.



Tracker



We have included perhaps one of the best trackers in India with this chatbot,thanks to covid19India.org for such a great initative.



Nearby Hospitals



Know the nearby testing and dedicated hospitals in your area incase of emergencies.List is shown as per user state



Know the myths



Tired of so many WhatsApp forwards regarding covid? want to know if it's real or just another made up news? we got you covered.
